 COMMENT:

  The wines were marvellous, mature and drinking extremely well.We believe
  that the lack of agreement was more a testament to the great overall
  qualityof the wines, with the youngest being 24 years old.

  The tasting was followed by two 100 point wines: the 1990 Hermitage La
  Chapelle and the 1989 Chave Hermitage. The Chave '89 could well have been
  part of the earlier tasting, with the caveat that the '89 was a little
  fresher. The '90 was a league apart. Still with a deep color and soft
  tannins. A brief vote confirmed that we had a 7/8 preference for the
  La Chapelle. Both were delicious. The chocolate dessert was a triumph to
  finish off a great tasting.
