Beverage Tasting Institute
An interesting website is the home page of the Beverage Testing Institute, on of the oldest
organized wine tasting enterprises in the US. The BTI's webmaster, Craig Goldwyn,
is also one of the US's most experienced wine journalists.

Local Wine Events.com

world. Beer and Spirits Events as well. Submit your Wine or Spirits related event.
