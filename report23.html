 COMMENT:

  The second flight of wines consisted of mixtures in which the Cabernet    
  Franc was mixed with Cabernet Sauvignon or Merlot or Malbec. The          
  percentages were Viader: Cabernet Franc 38%, Cabernet Sauvignon 62%;      
  Cheval Blanc: Cabernet Franc 66%, Merlot 33%, Malbec 1%;, L'Angelus:      
  Cabernet Franc 50%, Merlot 45%, Cabernet Sauvignon 5%.                    
  The Viader was a surprise winner over Cheval Blanc and L'Angelus. All but 
  two of the judges thought that the L'Angelus was the American wine; one   
  taster thought that the Cheval Blanc was the American wine because it was 
  more tannic that the Viader.
