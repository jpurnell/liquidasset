List of available back issues:

We have thirteen back issues of Liquid Assets available, and you may wish to add these to your collection. Here is what they contain:

Issue No. 13 (December 1997): New objective classification of the chateaux of Bordeaux. Buying the red wines of the Northern Rhone. How a wine competition really works. Analyzing a wine tasting the easy way.

Issue No. 12 (January 1996): A new and objective classification of California’s top cabernets. 1995, 1994, and 1993 Red Burgundy. 1995 Red Bordeaux. The wines of the Czech Republic.

Issue No. 11 (December 1994): Buying the wines of Chateauneuf-du-Pape and Australia: vintages, prices, visiting the producers. The whole red Bordeaux story: the 1994, 1993, and earlier vintages.

Issue No. 10 (December 1993): Buying the wines of the Northern Rhone (Cote Rotie, Hermitage, Cornas - and tips on visiting the vineyards). Red Burgundy from 1992, 1991 and before.

Issue No. 9 (December 1992): The ultimate review of Parker's and Broadbent's books on Bordeaux: What do they agree (and disagree) about and what effect do they have on the market? Vintage advice for 1991 and 1992 red Bordeaux. Bringing your own wines to our National Parks.

Sauternes: Auction Prices, Chateaux, and Vintage Advice. Vintage Advice: Red Bordeaux, 1983-1986. (Sold out, but photocopy available.)
