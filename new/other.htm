Beverage Tasting Institute

An interesting website is the home page of the Beverage Testing Institute, one of the oldest organized wine tasting enterprises in the US. The BTI's webmaster, Craig Goldwyn, is also one of the US's most experienced wine journalists.

Local Wine Events.com

Check it out at: http://www.LocalWineEvents.com A collective listing of Food and Wine Events in most major cities around the world. Beer and Spirits Events as well. Submit your Wine or Spirits related event.
